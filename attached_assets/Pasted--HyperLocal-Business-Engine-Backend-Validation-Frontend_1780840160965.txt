# HyperLocal Business Engine – Backend Validation & Frontend Readiness Audit

The backend implementation phase is considered feature-complete.

Your task is NOT to build new functionality.

Your task is to perform a comprehensive production-readiness audit and provide evidence for every claim.

## Objective

Prove that the backend is genuinely ready for frontend development.

Do not provide summaries without evidence.

For every completed item include:

* Commands executed
* Relevant logs
* HTTP requests/responses
* SQL query results
* Test output
* Any warnings or failures encountered
